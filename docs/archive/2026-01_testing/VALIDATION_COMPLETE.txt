KEY LEARNINGS
================================================================================

1. Walk-Forward Validation is ESSENTIAL
   - Successfully caught S4 overfitting (would have deployed broken strategy)
   - Multiple windows (15) provide robust evidence vs single OOS test
   - Should be MANDATORY for all production configs

2. Multi-Objective Optimization is NOT a Silver Bullet
   - S1 succeeded (1.5% degradation)
   - S4 failed (70.7% degradation)
   - Training data quality and size matter MORE than optimization method

3. Training Data Size Matters
   - S1: 18 months training → robust generalization
   - S4: 6 months training → severe overfitting
   - Recommendation: 18+ months of DIVERSE market conditions

4. Parameter Complexity Increases Overfitting Risk
   - S4: 6 parameters with wide ranges → overfitted
   - S1: 4 parameters with tighter ranges → robust
   - Recommendation: 3-4 parameters max for production

5. Regime Consistency is Critical
   - Archetypes MUST perform as designed in target regimes
   - S4 failing in bear markets (target regime) is RED FLAG
   - Indicates either regime classifier issue or signal logic flaw
