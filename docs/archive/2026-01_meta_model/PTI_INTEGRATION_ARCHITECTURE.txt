THRESHOLD RATIONALE
===================

pti_score > 0.60
----------------
▪ Conservative threshold - only high-conviction traps
▪ Avoids false positives on weak/noisy signals
▪ Ensures meaningful trap patterns detected

pti_confidence > 0.70
---------------------
▪ High confidence requirement - model must be certain
▪ Reduces risk of acting on uncertain predictions
▪ Aligns with production-grade risk management

S5 Boost Factor: 1.50x
-----------------------
▪ Moderate boost - not too aggressive
▪ Reflects increased conviction when retail is trapped
▪ Can be tuned based on live performance
